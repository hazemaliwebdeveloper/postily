# POZMIXAL COOKIE POLICY

**Effective Date:** November 25, 2025

## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

## 2. HOW WE USE COOKIES
We use cookies for:
*   **Essential:** Authentication and security (e.g., keeping you logged in).
*   **Analytics:** Understanding how you use our site (e.g., PostHog).
*   **Preferences:** Remembering your language and settings.

## 3. MANAGING COOKIES
You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using parts of the Service.

## 4. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES
We may use third-party services (like Stripe or Intercom) that set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.
