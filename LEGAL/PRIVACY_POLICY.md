# POZMIXAL PRIVACY POLICY

**Effective Date:** November 25, 2025

## 1. INTRODUCTION
Pozmixal ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. We operate in strict compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and SOC2 Type II standards.

## 2. DATA COLLECTION
We collect the following categories of data:
*   **Account Data:** Name, email, billing information.
*   **Usage Data:** Log files, device info, interaction metrics.
*   **Social Data:** OAuth tokens and profile data from connected social platforms (e.g., X, LinkedIn) solely for the purpose of publishing content.

## 3. DATA PROCESSING & PURPOSE
We process data to:
*   Provide and maintain the Service.
*   Process payments (via Stripe).
*   Monitor usage and detect security anomalies.
*   Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. DATA SHARING
We do **not** sell your personal data. We share data only with:
*   **Service Providers:** Cloud hosting (AWS), payment processors (Stripe), analytics (PostHog). All providers are vetted for SOC2 compliance.
*   **Legal Authorities:** If required by law.

## 5. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR & CCPA)
You have the right to:
*   **Access:** Request a copy of your data.
*   **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data.
*   **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
*   **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured format.

## 6. SECURITY
We implement enterprise-grade security measures, including encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3), regular penetration testing, and strict access controls.

## 7. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
Data may be processed in the United States. We utilize Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for data transfers from the EU/EEA.
