# POZMIXAL ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY (AUP)

**Effective Date:** November 25, 2025

## 1. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
You may not use Pozmixal to:
*   **Spam:** Send unsolicited bulk messages or spam.
*   **Illegal Content:** Distribute content that is illegal, harmful, threatening, or abusive.
*   **Malware:** Distribute viruses, worms, or other malicious code.
*   **Phishing:** Attempt to steal sensitive information from others.
*   **Botting:** Use automated scripts to manipulate the platform or social networks in violation of their terms.

## 2. SOCIAL PLATFORM COMPLIANCE
You must comply with the Terms of Service of all connected social platforms (e.g., X, LinkedIn, Facebook). Violation of their terms may result in suspension of your Pozmixal account.

## 3. ENFORCEMENT
We reserve the right to investigate and suspend or terminate accounts that violate this AUP. We may also report illegal activities to law enforcement.
